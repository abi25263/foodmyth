Parallax | Original Source Rellax is a really easy parallax framework. Simply give an element the class of 'rellax' and a data-rellax-speed="x", where the number range goes from -10 to +10. Positive numbers scroll upwards and negative numbers scroll down the page. The farther the number is from zero, the faster it travels.
